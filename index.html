Case Study

Inside Toyzie AI.

How a camera-first scanning app reached 100K+ downloads and the Top 30 of the US App Store's Reference category.

THE CHALLENGE

Identify anything, instantly

Collectors needed to point their camera at a toy and instantly know what it is, how rare it is, and what it's worth — without typing model numbers or digging through forums. That demanded fast, reliable on-device capture feeding an AI recognition layer.

WHAT I BUILT

Camera-to-value pipeline

I built the real-time AVFoundation capture flow and a SwiftUI interface in an MVVM architecture, wired to a Cloudflare Workers backend handling recognition and live market-value lookups — and owned the full App Store Connect → TestFlight → release cycle.

THE IMPACT

100K+ downloads, Top 30

Toyzie crossed 100,000 downloads in the US, climbed into the Top 30 of the App Store's Reference category, and holds 4.6★ across 2.1K ratings — and the same scan-value-track engine went on to power CD Scanner and QuitJunk AI.
